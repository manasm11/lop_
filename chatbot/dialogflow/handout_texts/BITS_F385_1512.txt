Course Title : Introduction to Gender Studies

Scope and Objective of the C ourse: To develop the knowledge necessary for addressing issues of gender equity
and understanding gender roles. The course would help students get a greater insight into women’s rights, their role in
shaping political, social, economic and cultural institutions. Emphasis would be laid on understanding the dynamics of
gender relations in educational institutions, work and home. The course would further aim at gender sensitization in
the context of societal stratifications.
