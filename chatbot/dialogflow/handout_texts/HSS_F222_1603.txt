1. Scope and Objective of the course:

The objective of the course is to acquaint students with the nature and functioning of language and the role
it plays in the society. The fundamental linguistic concepts will be exemplified through English. Reference to
Indian languages will also be made whenever possible.

2. Learning Outcomes:
Having successfully completed all the modules of the course, students will be able to understand

the origin and process of language development and language study
the properties and important characteristics of a human language
the structure of a language

Noam Chomsky’s contribution in language studies

how meaning is derived

use of language in various contexts

and analyze a text

the role of human brain in language production and perception
the differences in language acquisition and language learning
how a language changes over a period of time

the relationship between language, society and culture

the types and use of sign languages
