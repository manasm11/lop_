1. Course Description: Creativity and Aesthetics; Overview of Approaches to Literature, Interpretation of
selected Works; Cinema; Understanding Drama: Theme, Character, Plot, Form; Understanding Fiction:
Setting, Point of View, Plot and Character, Understanding Short Fiction and Novel: Meaning and Message,
Style and Coherence; Understanding Comics; Understanding Cinema: Relationship of Form and Meaning,
Image Construction, Shots, Scenes, Sequences, Mise-en-Scene, Codes, Frames, Signs, Syntax; Sound:
Diegetic & Non-diegetic Sound, Silence; Linear and Non-linear Narrative Structure.

2. Scope and Objective of the Course: The course attempts to inculcate in students a critical insight required
to interpret a work of literature or cinema. To enable the students to imbibe such perception and critical
judgment, the subtle nuances of such works will be taught extensively. Students will also be introduced to
different forms, terminologies and trends prevalent in such artistic ventures to enable them to place a work of
art in proper context.
