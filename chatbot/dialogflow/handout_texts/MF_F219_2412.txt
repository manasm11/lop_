1. Course Description:

Introduction to operations management, product planning, forecasting, facilities location and layout, process planning
and design, performance measures and capacity planning, scheduling and controlling, material requirements planning
and Just-in-time systems, inventory control, human resource management, advances in operations management, case
studies and software application.

2. Scope and Objective of the C ourse:
To provide a good fundamental concept in operations management
To promote the importance of decision making in operations management
To study the decision making in design, planning and control of conversion process / manufacturing systems
To develop decision making skills in conversion process / manufacturing systems
To make proficient in manufacturing / operations management
At the end of this course, the students will be able to understand the importance of decision making process in
design, planning and control of manufacturing systems
e Atthe end of this course, the student will be able to apply the analytical skills for decision making in operations
management
