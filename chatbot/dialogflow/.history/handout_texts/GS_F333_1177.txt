1. Course Description: Public Administration is a course specifically designed for the undergraduate
students of BITS Pilani. Through this course, students get the chance to learn about Public Administration in
general and Indian Public Administration in particular. This course gives them the opportunity to learn
various definitions, approaches and practical aspects pertaining to the field of Public Administration.

2. Scope and Objective of the C ourse: The course covers the basic principles, definitions and approaches
of Public Administration and provides exposure to students to specific case studies in the Indian context.
The objective of the course is to make students aware of the complexities of the practice of Public
Administration in order to equip them with up-to-date knowledge of Public Administration in India. These
students with the knowledge of Public Administration will be better suited to discharge their civic duties
efficiently in their professional capacities.

3. Text Books: Sharma M.P., Public Administration in Theory and Practice. Allahabad, Kitab Mahal, 40th
Ed., 2004.
4, Reference Books: Journal papers, videos and newspaper reports will be given in advance as supplementary

reference/reading materials. Students should be prepared to take initiatives for maximizing their learning
curve.
